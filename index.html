Our Culinary Vision
"Thai food is about balance and emotion. At Savor, we present those emotions through refined technique and modern expression."

— Chef Art Prasopchok Trakulphat

Tradition Honoured

Rooted in Thai culinary traditions dating to the 13th century, every dish at Savor begins with deep respect for authentic ingredients, regional flavour profiles, and the cultural stories they carry.

Technique Elevated

Chef Art's mastery of molecular gastronomy unlocks aromas and textures that classical Thai cooking rarely explores — sous-vide roulades, tamarind sorbets, egg foams that dissolve on the palate.

Presence Demanded

Dining at Savor is dinner as theatre. The open kitchen, intentional plating, and coursed tasting menu transform a meal into a story told plate by plate, spice by spice.

The Artist Behind the Plate

Iron Chef.
Thai Culinary
Visionary.

Chef Art brings to Toronto what Thailand's most storied kitchens produced over decades: a profound understanding of balance, spice, and the emotional resonance of food. His approach — described as Asian-twist molecular gastronomy — is not technique for technique's sake, but technique in service of flavour, story, and memory.

At Savor, he has created one of Toronto's first Thai-focused tasting menus, a format that allows the full arc of Thai gastronomy to be told — from ancient regional recipes to his avant-garde reimaginings — in a single, unforgettable evening.
